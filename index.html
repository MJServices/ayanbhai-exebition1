The BMW M3 is a high-performance sports car based on the BMW 3 Series. It has been produced since 1986 by BMW's M division. Known for its balance of speed, it features powerful engines, including inline-6 and V8 options in different generations, and offers manual or automatic transmissions. The M3 is a symbol of performance and luxury.

The BMW X5 is a luxury midsize SUV that combines premium comfort, advanced technology, and sporty performance. It was introduced in 1999 as BMW's first SUV (or SAV ‚Äì Sports Activity Vehicle, as BMW refers to it) and has since become one of the most popular vehicles in its class.

The BMW Z4 is a luxury two-seater roadster that combines sporty performance, elegant design, and open-top driving pleasure. It has been a staple in BMW's lineup since its debut in 2002, offering a blend of dynamic handling and comfort.

The BMW i7 is BMW's flagship all-electric luxury sedan, introduced as part of the 7 Series lineup. Combining advanced electric powertrains, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled luxury, the i7 showcases BMW's commitment to a sustainable, high-performance future.

The BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car that represents the pinnacle of performance, innovation, and sustainability. First introduced in 2014, it blends the best of both worlds: breathtaking acceleration from its hybrid powertrain and futuristic design that turns heads wherever it goes.

The BMW 5 Series is a luxury executive sedan that has set the benchmark for performance, comfort, and innovation in the mid-size sedan segment. Renowned for its refined design, dynamic driving experience, and advanced technology, the 5 Series is designed for those who demand sophistication with exceptional driving pleasure.
